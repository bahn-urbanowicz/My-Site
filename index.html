EverQuest NEXT Hype

EQ NEXT Hype is a fan-site that will be covering all the awesome content for Sony Online Entertainment's (SOE) new free to play MMORPG.

The Sauce

What is EverQuest NEXT?

EverQuest NEXT is an MMORPG (Massively Multi-Player Online Role Playing Game) that is being developed by SOE which will be free to play introducing new content such as permanent change, 8 classes to choose from and 40 to discover, destructibility, landmarks and consequences.

Gameplay Experience

Create a hero and fight back evil throughout the land of Norrath. SOE has made many changes to this next generation of EverQuest some of which include a new fighting system, voxels in landscapes making everything essentially destructible, layers throughout terrain increasing exploration and even provides you with tools to use voxels yourself to leave your mark in the land of Norrath.
